Platform Features

Your online presence. Your bookings.
All in one place.

BusyBay gives Australian automotive workshops a branded public page customers can find and book from — plus a simple dashboard to manage every job. No web developer required.

🌐

Your Workshop Page

Get a branded public page at busybay.com.au/your-workshop. Show your services, hours, location, and a "Book Now" button — customers find you and book without needing to call.

🔧

Showcase Your Services

List every service you offer — logbook, brakes, tyres, roadworthy, and more — with pricing and estimated duration. Customers know exactly what to book before they arrive.

✅

You Confirm Every Job

Every booking starts as a Pending Request. You review, confirm, reschedule, or decline — keeping you in full control of your bay capacity and workload.

🚗

Automotive-Specific Intake

Customers submit make, model, year, rego, and job description upfront. No more back-and-forth before you can quote or schedule the work.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Get notified the moment a booking lands. Confirm with one tap. Customers receive automatic emails — booking received, confirmed, and a reminder before their appointment.

👥

Customer Management

Every customer who books through your page is saved. See their vehicle history, past bookings, and contact details — all in one place without a separate CRM.

About BusyBay

More than bookings — your workshop's online home

BusyBay is purpose-built for small Australian automotive businesses — mechanical workshops, tyre shops, detailers, mobile mechanics, and roadworthy stations. We give each workshop a branded public page and a booking management dashboard, so they can operate professionally online without needing a web developer or expensive software.

Too many great workshops are invisible online. Customers can't find their services, can't book after hours, and end up going elsewhere. BusyBay fixes that — giving every workshop a professional online page, a booking system, and a customer record — all for a price small businesses can actually afford.

🌐

Your Own Workshop Page

Every workshop gets a branded public page showing their services, hours, and location. Customers find you, browse your services, and book — all without leaving your page.

🏁

Workshop in Control

Every booking is a request first — you confirm, reschedule, or decline. Designed to match how small automotive businesses actually operate.

💰

Priced for Small Business

Subscription plans from A$49/month — transparent, affordable pricing designed for sole traders and small workshop teams, not enterprise budgets.
