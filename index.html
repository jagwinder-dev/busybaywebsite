Platform Features

Everything your business needs
under one roof

No more juggling five different apps. BusyBay gives you the tools to run and grow your business — all in one place, all working together.

📅

Smart Bookings

Let customers book your services 24/7 with automated scheduling, reminders, and calendar sync. Reduce no-shows and stay organised.

🛒

Marketplace Listings

List your products and services on the BusyBay marketplace to reach thousands of local customers actively searching for what you offer.

💵

Payments & Invoicing

Accept payments online, in-person, or via invoice. BusyBay handles GST calculations, receipts, and reconciliation automatically.

👥

Customer Management

Build lasting relationships with a built-in CRM. Track history, notes, preferences, and automate follow-ups to keep customers coming back.

📈

Real-Time Analytics

Make smarter decisions with live dashboards showing revenue, bookings, customer trends, and business performance at a glance.

📱

Mobile First

Run your business from anywhere. Our mobile app gives you full control whether you're in the office, on-site, or on the go.

About BusyBay

Built by Australians, for Australian businesses

BusyBay was founded with a single mission: to give every Australian small business access to the same powerful tools that big companies take for granted — without the complexity or the price tag.

We understand the unique challenges of running a business in Australia — from local compliance and GST to the need for genuine community connection. BusyBay isn't just a software product; it's a platform built on local knowledge, designed to help you compete and thrive.

Whether you're a tradie in Brisbane, a wellness practitioner in Melbourne, or a boutique retailer in Perth — BusyBay is your business HQ.

🌟

Simplicity First

Technology should work for you, not against you. We obsess over making every feature easy and intuitive.

🤝

Community Driven

Every feature we build starts with real feedback from Australian business owners. You shape our roadmap.

🔒

Security & Trust

Your business data is protected with bank-grade security and stored on Australian servers. Full stop.
