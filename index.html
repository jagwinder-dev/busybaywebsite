Platform Features

Everything your workshop needs
to take bookings online

Stop losing jobs to missed calls and unanswered texts. BusyBay puts your workshop online with tools built specifically for Australian automotive businesses.

📅

24/7 Online Booking

Customers can book any time — day or night. Your personalised booking page captures service type, vehicle details, and rego number before you pick up the phone.

✅

You Confirm Every Job

Every booking starts as a Pending Request. You review, confirm, reschedule, or decline — keeping you in full control of your bay capacity and workload.

🏭

Your Brand, Your Page

Your BusyBay booking page is branded to your workshop — your logo, your colours, your services. Customers book with you, not through a marketplace.

🚗

Automotive-Specific Intake

Built for workshops — customers submit make, model, year, rego, and job description upfront. No more back-and-forth before you can quote or schedule.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Get notified the moment a booking lands. Confirm with one tap. Customers receive automatic emails — booking received, confirmed, and reminder before their appointment.

📱

Manage on the Go

View today's jobs, confirm requests, and update booking status from your phone — whether you're under a car or out on the road.

About BusyBay

A booking platform built for the workshop, not the marketplace

BusyBay is a B2B SaaS platform purpose-built for small Australian automotive service businesses — mechanical workshops, tyre shops, detailers, mobile mechanics, auto-electricians, and roadworthy inspection stations.

We exist because too many great workshops are still running on phone calls, text messages, and paper diaries. BusyBay gives them a simple, branded online booking presence — a link, a QR code, a dashboard — at a price small businesses can actually afford.

Unlike marketplace platforms that put your competitors on the same page, BusyBay is your workshop's own tool. You own the customer relationship. Your booking page is branded to you. We just handle the technology.

🆃

Workshop in Control

Every booking is a request first — you confirm, reschedule, or decline. Designed to match how small automotive businesses actually operate.

🏭

Your Brand, Not Ours

Customers book directly with your workshop, not through a BusyBay marketplace. Your name, your logo, your relationship.

💰

Priced for Small Business

Subscription plans from A$49/month — transparent, affordable pricing designed for sole traders and small workshop teams, not enterprise budgets.
